# Future Scope & Enhancements

## Short-term Improvements (3-6 months)
### Model Enhancements
- **Expand Dataset**: Include more trash varieties and conditions
- **Multi-language Support**: Localize classifications for global use
- **Confidence Calibration**: Improve probability scoring accuracy
- **Additional Classes**: Add sub-categories (PET, HDPE plastics, etc.)

### Application Features
- **History Tracking**: Log classified items with timestamps
- **Educational Content**: Add recycling tips and information
- **Share Functionality**: Allow users to share results
- **Offline Database**: Local storage of classification history

## Medium-term Goals (6-12 months)
### Technical Advancements
- **Object Detection**: Bounding boxes for multiple items in frame
- **Sensor Fusion**: Combine with other sensors for better accuracy
- **Cloud Sync**: Optional cloud backup and model updates
- **iOS Version**: Expand to Apple ecosystem

### User Experience
- **AR Overlay**: Augmented reality labels on detected items
- **Voice Feedback**: Audio announcements of classifications
- **Gamification**: Points system for correct recycling
- **Community Features**: User contributions and challenges

## Long-term Vision (1-2 years)
### Platform Expansion
- **Smart Bin Integration**: Connect with IoT waste bins
- **Municipal Scale**: Partner with waste management services
- **Educational Platform**: School and community programs
- **API Access**: Developer platform for third-party integrations

### Advanced AI Capabilities
- **Waste Condition Analysis**: Detect contamination or damage
- **Recycling Value**: Estimate recycling economic value
- **Trend Analytics**: Aggregate data for waste pattern analysis
- **Predictive Modeling**: Forecast waste generation patterns

## Research Directions
- **Few-shot Learning**: Adapt to new trash types with minimal data
- **Cross-domain Adaptation**: Transfer learning to different environments
- **Explainable AI**: Provide reasoning for classifications
- **Federated Learning**: Privacy-preserving model improvements

## Impact Scaling
- **Global Deployment**: Support for multiple regions and waste systems
- **Policy Integration**: Data for waste management policy decisions
- **Corporate Partnerships**: Integration with product manufacturers
- **Environmental Impact Tracking**: Quantify recycling improvements
